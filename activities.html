Activities & Impact

From community events to collaborative research sprints, our initiatives strengthen Jordan's cybersecurity ecosystem and nurture future-ready talent.

Milestones Timeline

Discover the journey of CyberHubJo through highlights of trainings, partnerships, and strategic projects across the Kingdom.

March 2024

The spark

Hatem Mosa pitched the CyberHubJo vision to fellow PhD students, planting the seed for a collaborative cybersecurity collective.

March 2025

Momentum from PSUT leadership

Following an inspiring conversation with the PSUT presidency, the founding members aligned on announcing CyberHubJo to the wider community.

April 2025

Vision building sessions

Weekly workshops kicked off to refine the initiative's mission, values, and impact areas while shaping the brand identity.

May 2025

Knowledge sharing begins

Members started hosting recurring meetups to exchange ideas and deliver hands-on cybersecurity trainings every week.

June 2025

LinkedIn community launch

The CyberHubJo LinkedIn group opened to the public, creating a digital hub to celebrate achievements and connect with partners across the globe.

July 2025

First in-person gathering

The team met face-to-face to workshop their debut research paper, strengthen collaboration, and realign responsibilities.

October 2025

Website debut

CyberHubJo's official website went live, providing a central destination for activities, publications, and community updates.
