January 2026 ✈️ International Event

Cybersecurity for Research Infrastructures — AUB Cyprus

CyberHubJo, represented by Hatem Mosa and Amro Saleh, participated in a cybersecurity workshop at the American University of Beirut in Cyprus. The event was organized by SESAME Jordan with participation from CERN (Switzerland) and the Cyprus Research and Academic Network (CYNET), bringing together global experts for engaging and fruitful discussions.
